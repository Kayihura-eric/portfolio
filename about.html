About me

I am a passionate Software Engineering student at the Adventist University of Central Africa (AUCA), building a strong foundation in programming, problem-solving, and modern development practices. My skills have been further strengthened through professional courses from SoloLearn, UniAthena, and Makerspace, as well as early exposure to computing in high school. I have experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Oracle Database, and I enjoy designing intuitive user interfaces using Figma. I’m driven by the goal of creating meaningful digital solutions that improve everyday life—especially tools that support communities and real-world challenges. I love learning, experimenting with new technologies, and building projects that push me to grow both technically and creatively. Outside of coding, I enjoy collaborating, sharing knowledge, and contributing to impactful ideas.
